About us

GOA - Where future technologies meet today's education

👁️

Our vision

We are Georgia's leading technology education center, nurturing the next generation of IT specialists and contributing to the country's digital development.

🎯

Our mission

Our mission is to provide all students with affordable and quality technology education that will enable them to successfully work in the digital economy.

Our history

2020

Establishment

GOA was founded by 3 enthusiastic developers who wanted to share their knowledge with a new generation.

2021

First courses

Launch of the Web Development course with 25 students. 90% of the first graduates were employed in the IT field.

2022

Expansion

Addition of Game Development and Graphic Design programs. Number of students increased to 100.

2023

Tech Partners

Signing partnership agreements with 20+ international and local IT companies for student employment.

2024

New programs

Algorithms, Electrical Engineering, and game development courses added. Reaching 500+ graduates.

2025

Future

Launch of AI/ML specialization and corporate training programs. Preparing to enter the European market.
